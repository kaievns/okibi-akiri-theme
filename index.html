01 · PHILOSOPHY

The ash carries it

Okibi is built on restraint. The charcoal does the work; the ember is a spark, not a wash; supporting hues earn their place by carrying meaning. In Akari the ground inverts to paper — the discipline does not.

炭

The ash carries it

Around 85% of any surface is the neutral charcoal ramp and grey text. Colour is the exception; the base is the rule.

燠

The ember is a spark

One ember moment per view at rest — the thing in focus. Never body text, never a large fill, never two embers fighting.

意

Hue follows meaning

Green for good, red for wrong, blue for links and net. A supporting colour appears only when it signals something.

静

One voice at a time

Within a cluster, let the ember or a single hue lead. Chrome — level bars, idle icons, toggles — stays neutral.

The ember · 燠

The signature, and the only accent that touches UI chrome. Read it as heat — the live thing, the cursor, the place your attention should land. It earns that by appearing rarely.

Tuning zone — stay inside H 24–32° · S 56–66% · L 50–57%H 24–32° · S 64–74% · L 40–47%. Below it drifts brown; above it goes mandarin; brighter reads gold and gets loud.

Reserve the ember for
  • The active / focused workspace
  • The text cursor & current line
  • The editor mode indicator (NORMAL)
  • A focused input or URL bar
  • The active tab marker · primary heading
Never the ember for
  • Body text or long runs of type
  • Large fills, backgrounds or whole icons
  • Level bars (volume, battery) — go neutral
  • More than one element in view at rest
  • Decoration with no focus meaning
